Food and Drinks

Ocean Breeze Restaurant

If you're looking for a fantastic meal and an even better view, then Ocean Breeze Restaurant is for you. There are a variety of seafood dishes to choose from and the outdoor seating near the water offers a great eating experience.

Solstice Restaurant

Solstice is the perfect place to indulge in a fine dining setting. There are plenty of Italian dishes to choose from as well as several different wines. This is a restaurant your family will love.

Rico's Bar and Grill

Rico's Bar and Grill is known for its cold beer and cheese burgers. Its an awesome spot to grab something quick to eat or take a break from the sun and have a few drinks.

Swim-Up Bar

There's nothing like enjoying a tasty cocktail or refreshing frozen drink while soaking up the sun. You can sit at the bar while cooling off from the heat in the pool or float around with your drink, the choice is yours!
