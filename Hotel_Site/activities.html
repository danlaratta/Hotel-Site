Resort Activities

Water Sports

Experience the fun that the Bahama's waters has to offer by taking part in some water sports. Ride the mild waves in a kayak, banana boat, paddle board, and more!

Minature Golf

Spend time with the family and putt away on our minature golf course which has 15 holes!

Pool Volleyball

Three times throughout the day a net is stretched across the pool for a game of volleyball. All ages are welcome to play!

Beach

Soak up the sunshine and lounge out on the Resorts private beach. Stretch out in a lounge chair or spread a towel out on the warm sand.

Water Slides

Experience the adrenaline rush of speeding down one of our several choices of water slides and splashing into the pool down below!

Sports

Placed through out the resort are basketball and tennis courts for you and your family to get your game on!
